Naawi-Oodena

Naawi-Oodena is a new community being developed on the former Kapyong Barracks site in Winnipeg, Manitoba. The project is a collaboration between the Treaty One Development Corporation and the City of Winnipeg, and aims to create a vibrant, sustainable community that reflects the values and culture of the Anishinaabe people.



  • Residential

    Lower density

    The lower density areas will accommodate buildings no taller than 3.5 storeys.

    Medium density

    The medium-density areas will house buildings up to six or eight storeys.

  • Commercial Mixed-Use

    Commercial mixed-use areas will combine retail, office, and residential spaces, creating vibrant streetscapes and supporting a walkable community.

  • Community Spaces

    Community spaces will facilitate social interaction and participation by residents, such as parks, playgrounds, and event and activity meeting spaces.

  • Sports and Recreation

    Sports and recreation facilities will provide the possibility for physical activity, such as sports fields, courts, and fitness centers for all ages.

  • Cultural Campus + Education

    The cultural campus and education facilities will respect Anishinaabe culture and provide educational opportunities, such as schools, cultural centers, and meeting spaces.

  • Mixed-Use Village

    The multi-use village is a central location, integrating residential, commercial, and community uses to create a vibrant and accessible neighborhood hub.
